## Why Marketing Breaks Down

Here's what happens today when a founder needs marketing:

**Step 1.** You spend 2 hours on a call explaining your business to an agency.

**Step 2.** They nod. They take notes. They say "got it."

**Step 3.** Two weeks later, they send you a draft that sounds like it was written for a completely different company.

**Step 4.** You spend another hour giving feedback. "No, that's not what I meant."

**Step 5.** They revise. It's closer. But it's still not *you*.

**Step 6.** Three rounds later, you settle for "good enough" because you're exhausted and you have a business to run.

That's not a bad agency. That's **human communication bandwidth noise**. Every time information passes from your brain to someone else's brain through conversation, meaning degrades. Context leaks. Nuance disappears.

Now multiply that by every freelancer, every designer, every copywriter. Every single handoff is a lossy compression of what you actually meant.
